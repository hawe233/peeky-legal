Privacy Policy — Peeky

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Peeky ("Peeky", "we", "us", or "the app") is a dual-camera photo and video app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using Peeky you agree to this policy.

1. Information we handle

  • Photos and videos you capture. Content you create is generated and stored locally on your device. It is only transmitted off your device if you choose to share, post (for example, to TikTok), or export it. The app may automatically remove locally stored captures after a few days.
  • TikTok account information. If you sign in with TikTok, we receive basic profile information from TikTok (such as your TikTok open ID, display name, and avatar) so you can log in and publish content you choose to post.
  • Location (optional). With your permission, the app may use your approximate or precise location to add a location/map sticker or to tag a capture. This is optional and controlled by the in-app setting and your iOS location permission.
  • Camera and microphone. Used to capture photos and videos. Audio/video is not recorded except as part of content you intentionally capture.
  • Device and usage data (via third-party SDKs). Third-party software development kits integrated in the app — including the TikTok SDK and related ByteDance frameworks — may collect device identifiers and technical, usage, and diagnostic data for functionality, security, and analytics, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. How information is used

  • To provide the camera experience (capture, filters, stickers, picture-in-picture, timestamp).
  • To enable TikTok login and the publishing/sharing of content you choose to post.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the app.

3. Third parties

Login and content posting are powered by TikTok's SDK. Information shared with TikTok / ByteDance is governed by their privacy policy: TikTok Privacy Policy. App distribution and beta testing are provided by Apple. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data retention and deletion

  • Captures remain on your device until you delete them or the app prunes them; deleting the app removes its local data.
  • To revoke the app's access to your TikTok account, remove its authorization in your TikTok account settings.

5. Permissions

The app requests Camera, Microphone, Photo Library, and Location access only when needed for a feature. You can review or change these at any time in iOS Settings.

6. Children

Peeky is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region) and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
